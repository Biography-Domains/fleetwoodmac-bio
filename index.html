Fleetwood Mac

Rock, Blues Rock, Pop Rock, Soft Rock

Fleetwood Mac is a British-American rock band, formed in London in 1967. The band has sold more than 120 million records worldwide, making them one of the world's best-selling bands. Fleetwood Mac began as a British blues band in 1967, led by guitarist Peter Green, drummer Mick Fleetwood, and bassist John McVie.
